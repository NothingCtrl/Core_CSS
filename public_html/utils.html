Core_Utils

Core_CSS contains various classes, objects, utilities and widgets to facilitate the development of your application. Such as:

  • Easing functions
  • Gravatar helper
  • Hash functions
  • Locales (coreLocales)
  • Pickers (corePickers)
  • Storage engine
  • Service classes
  • Template engine
  • Touch and swipe plugin
  • Utilities functions (coreUtils)
