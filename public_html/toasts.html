Toasts

Toasts are primarily used for system messaging. They also display at the bottom of the screen, but may not be swiped off-screen. Core_CSS provides simple methods to create toasts.

Core_CSS define two object to create toasts: $.Toasts and coreToasts with method create 

        <script>
            coreToasts.create(message, callback, timeout, class);
        </script>
    
        coreToasts.create('This is a toast')

        coreToasts.create('This is a toast', function(){
            alert('This is a callback of toast')
        })

        coreToasts.create('This is a toast', null, 5000)
