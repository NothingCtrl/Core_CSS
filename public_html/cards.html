Cards

Core_CSS provides any classes to create cards. A card is a sheet of material that serves as an entry point to more detailed information.

Cards may contain a photo, text, and a link about a single subject. They may display content containing elements of varying size, such as photos with captions of variable length.

Default

Card header
Card with header and footer. Card header is used to display card title and footer for some additional information or for custom actions.
